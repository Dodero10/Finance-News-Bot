FIGURE DESCRIPTIONS
==============================

accuracy_analysis.png:
  - Panel A: Overall accuracy across all queries
  - Panel B: Accuracy comparison between easy vs hard queries
  - Shows percentage of queries with perfect tool selection

precision_analysis.png:
  - Panel A: Overall precision across all queries
  - Panel B: Precision comparison between easy vs hard queries
  - Shows proportion of selected tools that were relevant

recall_analysis.png:
  - Panel A: Overall recall across all queries
  - Panel B: Recall comparison between easy vs hard queries
  - Shows proportion of relevant tools successfully identified

f1_analysis.png:
  - Panel A: Overall F1 score across all queries
  - Panel B: F1 score comparison between easy vs hard queries
  - Shows harmonic mean of precision and recall
